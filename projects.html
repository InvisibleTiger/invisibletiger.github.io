Functional Models

Cesna 152

An ingenious model of the Cessna 152, crafted from recycled tissue paper rolls, demonstrates creativity and sustainability. Featuring functional flaps, rudders, elevators, and ailerons, this eco-friendly creation embodies environmental consciousness while capturing the aircraft's essence with remarkable accuracy. Integrating recycled materials showcases resourcefulness, proving that sustainability and functionality can coexist in artful designs.

Lamborghini Sian

A striking cardboard model of the Lamborghini Sian features functional elements like steering and a differential, alongside iconic falcon-wing style doors that open with precision. Despite its unconventional material, the model exudes the brand's unmistakable aura of luxury and power from every angle, showcasing the ingenuity behind bringing automotive dreams to life.

Mobile Crane

A remarkable cardboard crane boasts precise controls over its actuators, utilizing meticulously calculated strings to lift loads upwards of 3 lbs with ease. Despite its humble material, this crane exemplifies ingenuity and functionality, showcasing the potential of cardboard engineering. With intricate design and careful construction, it demonstrates how unconventional materials can be harnessed to achieve remarkable results in both form and function.

HUMMER

An innovative cardboard HUMMER features a skeleton of sturdy cardboard strips supporting a thinner exterior body, effortlessly holding weights up to 10 lbs. This creative blend of form and function showcases the versatility of cardboard as a building material, highlighting ingenuity and craftsmanship, while inspiring eco-friendly engineering solutions. From its rugged appearance to its surprising strength, this cardboard marvel embodies the spirit of innovation, offering a glimpse into the future of sustainable vehicle design.
